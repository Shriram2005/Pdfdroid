Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2024

1. Introduction

At PdfDroid, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our online PDF tools.

2. Information We Process

2.1 PDF Documents

- All PDF processing is done locally in your browser

- We do not upload, store, or transmit your documents to any servers

- Your documents never leave your device

2.2 Usage Data

We collect minimal anonymous usage data including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Features used
  • General usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand how users interact with our tools
  • Develop new features based on user needs

4. Data Security

4.1. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

4.2. All processing is done client-side using modern web technologies.

4.3. We use HTTPS encryption for all communications.

5. Third-Party Services

We use limited third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for usage statistics
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) for better performance

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Request information about our data practices

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes through our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us.
